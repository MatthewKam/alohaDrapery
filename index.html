In the Hawaiian language, the translation of the word Aloha is "alo" (the presence or face to face) and "ha" (breath). In this spirit, AlohaDrapery.com is cultivating a worldwide effort to share its expertise in the manufacturing and delivering of custom sized fabric draperies at great value.
