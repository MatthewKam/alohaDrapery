Attached Lining

This is an additional piece of fabric that is attached to the back of a face fabric. The lining is usually intended for greater privacy, insulation, or protection for the face fabric against sun and heat damage. Both light-filtering and blackout lining are available. Refer to photo.

Carriers

These are plastic devised for hooks to be placed into. Carriers are also referred to as glides.

Characteristics of a fabric

Random slubs are an inherent quality of the fabric.

Cleaning Instructions

For all of our fabrics, although some are washable, it is recommended that your custom drapery be dry cleaned due to the crinoline used at the top of the drapery that helps to give the header strength and help to keep the shape of the pleats as well. Drapery can be maintained by vacuuming and brushing. Please refer to the Cleaning Instructions Card provided with your custom drapery.

Color variations

There can be slight color variations whenever dyeing drape material. The variations, if any, are usually marginal and often not detectable.

Fullness

The more fabric that is pleated into the drapery, the fuller the draperies become. The fullness of our drapery are at a 100% fullness (2 times the size of your track length).

Height

The height of the drape is the measurement from the top center of the rod or track down to where you would like your custom drapery to hang. If there is no rod, the height is the measurement from the top center of the window frame or casing to the bottom of where you would like it to hang.

Hooks

Drape hooks are used to connect the drape to the rod or track. The hooks are attached to the top back of the drape and behind the French Pleats. The hooks provided with your custom drapery are stainless steel.

Hook Settings

The placement of hooks on a drapery. For your custom drapery, the drapery hooks will be set at 1-1/2" from the top of the drapery. Although it may differ for different companies, a general rule is for Ceiling Mounted tracks, the drapery hooks are set at 1-1/4" and for Wall Mounted tracks, the hooks are set at 1-3/4" from the top of the drapery.
